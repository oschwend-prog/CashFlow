1. Who we are

CashFlow is operated by Azure IM Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Azure IM Ltd is the data controller responsible for your personal information under the UK General Data Protection Regulation ("UK GDPR") and the Data Protection Act 2018.

In this policy, "we", "us", "our" or "CashFlow" refers to Azure IM Ltd. "You" refers to the person whose personal information we collect — a waitlist signup, a future courier, a future requester, or a visitor to our website or app.

Our registered office and contact details are in section 12.

2. Data we collect

We collect different information depending on how you interact with CashFlow. The categories below reflect our current pre-launch state (waitlist mode). When we go operational, additional categories will apply (identity verification documents, banking details, transaction data) — we will update this policy accordingly before that happens.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages you view, buttons you tap, time spent. We use Vercel's built-in anonymous analytics; no individual user tracking.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen size, language. Used for diagnostics.
  • Approximate location — derived from your IP address at country/region level only. We do not collect precise location in waitlist mode.
  • Campaign attribution — if you arrived via a marketing link, the UTM parameters in the URL (source, medium, campaign).

2.3 Information from third parties

In waitlist mode we receive no data from third parties. When CashFlow goes operational, we will receive identity verification results from our KYC provider and transaction/payment status from our licensed payment partner. This section will be updated before any such processing begins.

5. International transfers

Some of our service providers are based outside the UK. Where your personal data is transferred to a country that the UK does not consider adequate, we rely on the UK's International Data Transfer Agreement or equivalent safeguards to ensure your information remains protected to UK GDPR standards.

6. How long we keep it

  • Waitlist signups — until CashFlow launches in your city and for up to 24 months after, or until you ask us to delete earlier.
  • Support emails — for 2 years after your most recent contact.
  • Accounting records — 6 years (UK HMRC requirement).
  • Fraud prevention records — up to 5 years where we have a legitimate interest in retaining them.

Once a retention period ends, we delete or anonymise the information.

7. Security

We take data security seriously. Measures include:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) for all connections
  • Encryption at rest for all database storage
  • Row-level security policies on our database so personal data is only accessible via authenticated server-side functions
  • Principle of least privilege for staff access
  • Regular security patching of all infrastructure

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach that poses a risk to you, we will notify you and the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) within 72 hours as required by law.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights. To exercise any of them, email privacy@cashflow.app.

  • Access — get a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on consent (e.g. marketing emails), you can withdraw at any time via the unsubscribe link or by emailing us
  • Automated decisions — we do not make automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects

We respond to requests within one month. For complex requests, we may extend by two further months and will let you know.

Deleting your waitlist signup

Email privacy@cashflow.app with the email address you signed up with and the subject line "Delete my data". We will confirm deletion within 7 working days.

9. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website uses a minimal set of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site to work (e.g. remembering your form state). These do not require your consent.
  • Analytics — anonymous, aggregated usage statistics via Vercel Analytics. No individual user profiling.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. If this ever changes we will update this policy and request consent where legally required.

10. Children

CashFlow is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected a child's data, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, the law, or our practices. The "last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the most recent change took effect. For material changes (such as the addition of new data categories when we go operational), we will notify waitlist members by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
