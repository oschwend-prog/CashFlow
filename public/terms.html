2. What CashFlow is

CashFlow is a peer-to-peer marketplace that connects people who want physical cash delivered to their door ("Requesters") with verified individuals who can deliver it ("Couriers"). We operate the platform, but we are not a party to the cash handovers themselves.

We do not hold customer funds. Payment processing is handled by a licensed third-party payments provider.

6. The marketplace model

When the Service is operational, the following principles apply:

  • Couriers are independent contractors, not employees, agents, or workers of Azure IM Ltd. Couriers are responsible for their own tax, National Insurance, and operating costs.
  • The cash handover contract is between Requester and Courier. CashFlow facilitates the match, the payment authorisation, and the dispute process, but is not a principal to the cash delivery.
  • The cash delivered is not CashFlow's cash. It is cash that a Courier lawfully holds. Couriers are responsible for the legitimate source of the cash they deliver.
  • CashFlow retains a platform fee from each completed delivery, paid from the delivery fee set between the parties. The current split is disclosed in-app at the time of each transaction.

7. Fees and payments

Pre-launch (waitlist), the Service is free to join. No fees apply until the Service goes operational.

Once operational, the fee structure at launch will be: a fixed delivery fee of £5 plus a variable fee set by the Courier (between 1% and 20% of the cash amount). CashFlow retains 25% of the combined fee as a platform fee; the Courier receives the remaining 75%. The exact amounts will be disclosed in-app before any transaction.

All payments are processed by our licensed payments partner. Payouts to Couriers are made to their nominated UK bank account via that partner.
